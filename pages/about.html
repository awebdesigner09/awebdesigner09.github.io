About me

Hey there! I'm Naga sai, a passionate engineer and AI enthusiast hailing from Andhra Pradesh and now calling Hyderabad, India, my home. With a Master's in Computer Applications under my belt since 2009, I've been on an exciting journey in the world of software engineering for the past 15 years.

My tech adventure kicked off in 2010 when I joined Sreeven Infocom as a software developer, cutting my teeth on state government projects. But I was just getting started! In 2012, I leveled up by joining Mindtree, where I got the incredible opportunity to work at Microsoft's campus as an SDET in the Windows Azure Backup team.

Since 2014, I've been part of the Capgemini family, dedicating my skills to Discover Financial Services. Here, I've been developing and maintaining their student loans applications, constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Throughout my career, I've worn many hats and worked with a diverse tech stack. From C# and ASP.NET to React JS and Web services, from JQuery to SQL Server, and even VB6 and Crystal reports, I've done it all. I've also got hands-on experience with Jenkins for continuous integration and delivery.

But here's the thing - I'm always hungry to learn more. That's why I've completed the Machine Learning specialization from Stanford, as well as Deep Learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP) specializations from DeepLearning.ai. I've even trained in fine-tuning Large Language Models (LLMs) with AWS.

Now, I'm looking to pivot my career towards Machine Learning and Generative AI Engineering roles. Why? Because I believe these fields are the future, and I want to be at the forefront of this exciting technological revolution.

So, that's me in a nutshell - a highly motivated, smart-working software engineer with a passion for AI and a never-ending thirst for knowledge. I'm always up for new challenges and ready to make my mark in the world of Machine Learning and Generative AI. Let's connect and see what amazing things we can create together!
